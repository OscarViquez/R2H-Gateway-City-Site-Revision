The Malecon

  • Free

  • Free

  • Free

  • Free

Malecon is an iconic mile long boardwalk / walkway that connects from Centro to Zona Romantica. Along the path, you will find an abundance of restaurants, sculptures, gallaries, and shops. On top of that, you would be taking in the scenic view of the beautiful Bay of Banderas. As night approaches, the area lights up and is bustling with many color bars and clubs.

In English, it is translated into the romantic zone. The city's historic district has vastly colorful buildings with contrasting cobblestone streets.
