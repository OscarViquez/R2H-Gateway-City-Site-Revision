The Malecon

  • Free

  • For Everyone

  • Outdoors

  • Food, Stores, Hotels

Malecon is an iconic mile long boardwalk / walkway that connects from Centro to Zona Romantica. Along the path, you will find an abundance of restaurants, sculptures, gallaries, and shops. On top of that, you would be taking in the scenic view of the beautiful Bay of Banderas. As night approaches, the area lights up and is bustling with many color bars and clubs.

Zona Romantica

  • Free

  • For Everyone

  • Outdoors

  • Marketplace, Food

In English, it is translated into the romantic zone. The city's historic district has vastly colorful buildings with contrasting cobblestone streets. Besides the amazing restaurants, art galleries, and beaches, you will find the iconic Olas Altas Farmers Market. They sell locally grown and fresh produce, local artisan products, and more!
